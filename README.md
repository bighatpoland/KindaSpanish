# Kinda Spanish

Kinda Spanish is a mobile-first app for adults living in Spain who would like to speak Spanish... but also would like to avoid speaking to people whenever possible.

It prepares you for unavoidable real-life situations - like the courier waiting downstairs, the cashier asking a question you didn't expect, or your neighbor starting a conversation you did not emotionally schedule.

Instead of perfect grammar, it focuses on saying something that works. Ideally on the first try. Real phrases, short audio practice, and tiny wins you can fit between overthinking and pretending you understood everything.

There are streaks, levels, and small rewards to trick your brain into coming back. Progress is measured in slightly fewer awkward pauses and a growing ability to react before your mind goes blank.

The goal is simple: freeze less, respond faster, and survive more conversations in Spanish.
