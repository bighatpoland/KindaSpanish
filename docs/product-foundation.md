# Product Foundation

## Core promise

Speak good enough Spanish in everyday life without waiting to be perfect.

## Main user

An adult living in Spain who wants short, mobile-first practice for queues, walks, errands, and tiny social moments.

## Pillars

1. Real-life scenarios with immediate carryover into daily life
2. Arcade loops that reward recall, listening, and speaking retries
3. Progress systems that celebrate useful behavior instead of passive tapping

## Mobile rules

- One-hand friendly navigation
- Short sessions under two minutes for a single run
- Audio first, reading second
- Fast restarts after interruption
